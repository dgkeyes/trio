TRIO survey analyses 2018

TRIO survey analyses 2018

1 Introduction

This is a report on surveys conducted after online TRIO training in March 2018. This training was conducted by Cuyahoga Community College. More infomation available here. As of April 11, 54 (87% of those who initially registered) have successfully completed the training.

Of the 62 participants registered for the training, 45 completed the pre survey (a 73% response rate) and 42 completed the post survey (a 68% response rate).
